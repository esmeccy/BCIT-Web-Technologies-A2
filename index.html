About Soma & Soil

Soma & Soil app helps you slow down, reconnect with your senses, and bring deeper meaning to your candle experience. Inspired by the chinese ancient philosophy of Wuxing, it helps you reconnect with your emotions through elemental energy, intentional reflection, and simple guided rituals. Whether you’re starting your day, winding down, or simply creating a quiet moment, the app is your digital companion for restoring balance.

Features

Scan Your Candle

Scan your Soma & Soil candle to unlock its elemental meaning and access a matching ritual designed to align with its scent and energy.

Mood Check-Ins

Gently pause and reflect with daily check-ins. Track your emotional rhythm and let your mood guide you toward the element and ritual you need most.

Ritual Mode

Select how you feel, and the app recommends a guided ritual inspired by the five elements of Wuxing. Light your candle and follow calming breathwork, reflections, and ambient soundscapes to restore presence and balance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to own a Soma & Soil candle to use the app?

No, but pairing the app with our candles enhances the full ritual experience.

Are the rituals religious or spiritual?

They are nature-inspired and element-based, designed to support emotional balance and mindfulness.

Is this app for meditation?

It’s not traditional meditation. it’s a sensory experience rooted in breath, scent, and natural rhythm.

Is it free?

Yes, the app is free with optional premium rituals and content.
